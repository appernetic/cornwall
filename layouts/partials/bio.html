--- title: about Häusle --- Häusle in Cornwall is the title of a book written by Elisabeth Kabatek and means 'little house' in German. We decided to call our Cornish cottage in Redruth, just a few miles from where the story takes place, Häusle to market it as a holiday destination for our German holidaymakers.
